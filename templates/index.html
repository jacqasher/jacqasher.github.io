About

Hi I'm Jacqueline! Welcome. I love watercolor illustration and graphic design. This space combines the two and allows me to share my creations with you. Every piece incorporates my own hand illustrations, crafted to fit the design of the final piece.

Utilizing my own graphic design I incorporate those illustrations into bespoke designs. We hope you find something you love, or follow me on Instagram or Pinterest to keep up with my latest creations!
